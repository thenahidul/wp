How is SENT different from file storage & sharing sites like Dropbox?
SENT enables encrypted file transfer, we send files -- we don't store them for extended periods of time, our limit is 5 days, and we don't provide unsecure internet links to share. Your file is sent to whom you intend to send it and is then deleted from our infrastructure. That is it.
